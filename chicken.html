Kentucky Fried Chicken

Description

This is a very basic buttermilk fried chicken recipe that you can make with just the ingredients in your house

Fried Chicken Recipes

  • Four pounds of chicken legs
  • One Cup Of Buttermilk
  • Two cups of all purpose flour
  • One tablespoon of paprika
  • A dash of salt & pepper
  • Two quarts of vegetable oil for cooking

Direction

  1. Mix all purpose flower,salt and pepper and paprika into one bowl
  2. Mix your chicken legs into the mix and cover chicken legs well
  3. take the chicken legs and bath them in the buttermilk until covered
  4. take the freshly cover buttermilk chicken legs and put some more powder mix on chicken legs
  5. Now pour vegetable oil into a cooking and get oil hot to get ready and cook the chicken
  6. Now Put the chicken legs in to the boiling oil and cook chicken until golden brown
